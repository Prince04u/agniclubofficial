Account &amp; access

This FAQ collects the most frequent questions our support team receives. If your question isn’t here, email support@agniclub.com and we’ll add it. Every answer below is reviewed by the operations team monthly to stay accurate.

Most account issues fall into one of four buckets: AgniClub login, AgniClub register / signup, gift code redemption, or wallet credits (deposit bonus, daily salary, referral super bonus). Each bucket has its own dedicated page on this site with deeper walkthroughs; the questions below are quick-reference summaries.

The fastest path to a resolution is almost always a short, specific email with your AgniClub user ID and a single screenshot. Long, multi-issue tickets take longer to triage because they need to be split across multiple specialist queues.

Rewards &amp; bonuses

The AgniClub reward system is intentionally transparent. Every tier, every threshold and every payout is published on this website. If a bonus you expected didn’t arrive, the first step is to compare your activity against the published tables on the homepage. The most common cause of a “missing” bonus is a deposit that fell just below the next tier or a referral that hasn’t yet completed the minimum betting volume.

Bonus credits are automatic and instant for deposit bonuses, end-of-day for daily salary, and triggered by completion of the bonus criteria for the referral super bonus and ads-fee support. If you wait more than the normal window and still see nothing, raise a ticket with the exact timestamp of the qualifying activity.

Gift codes are credited within a second of pressing Redeem. If a code returns “invalid”, it has either expired, hit its cap or already been used on your account — move on to the next one rather than retrying the same code.

Extra questions members ask less often but care deeply about

Can I change my AgniClub mobile number? Yes, but only via the security desk and only after identity verification. The change typically completes in 24–48 hours. The slow speed is deliberate — mobile-number change is the single most common attack vector against prediction-platform accounts, so we err heavily on the side of safety.

Does AgniClub support multiple bank accounts? Each AgniClub account binds to one bank account at a time. You can change the bound bank, but again only after identity verification. Multi-bank binding is a frequently requested feature; we’ve decided against shipping it because the fraud cost outweighs the convenience benefit.

What happens to my balance if I stop using the platform? Your balance stays in your wallet, untouched, until you withdraw it. There is no inactivity fee, no expiry on real-money balance, and no penalty for taking a break. Bonus balance follows whatever expiry was published with the specific bonus.

Can I gift another member my bonus balance? No. Bonus balance is non-transferable by design — it’s tied to the account that earned it. Real-money balance can be withdrawn to your bound bank and then sent however you like.

How transparent is AgniClub about losing streaks? Your wallet history shows every round, every stake and every outcome with exact timestamps. There is no hidden ‘suggested next bet’ engine pushing you to increase stakes. We do not hide losses; we surface them so you can stay disciplined.

How to escalate an AgniClub issue when the first reply isn’t enough

Most tickets are resolved on the first reply. When they aren’t, the escalation path is straightforward. Reply to the same thread (don’t open a new ticket — duplicates push you to the back of the queue) and add the words ‘requesting escalation’ at the top. That short phrase routes the thread to a senior agent on the next pass through the queue.

If the issue is time-sensitive — a stuck withdrawal, a security incident, a frozen account — add the timestamp of when the issue started and a single line describing the business impact (for agents, that means the affected team size and the relevant bonus tier). Senior agents triage by impact, not by complaint length.

If 48 hours pass with no movement, escalate again with the original ticket ID and a one-line summary. Persistent, professional follow-up always beats angry resending. Our support culture is built around respect; that respect runs both ways.

AgniClub responsible play commitments

Color prediction is meant to be entertainment, not income replacement. AgniClub publishes its responsible-play tooling directly inside the app and inside every reward page on this site so members are never more than a tap away from setting a healthy boundary. Personal deposit limits, session-length caps, daily cool-down windows and self-exclusion periods are all configurable from Profile → Responsible Play. None of these tools cost anything; none of them affect bonus eligibility; all of them can be tightened at any time without staff approval.

Our internal anti-fraud and welfare systems also work in the background. The platform flags abnormal betting curves, repeated late-night marathon sessions and sharp swings in deposit behaviour. When a flag triggers, our welfare desk reaches out privately with a soft check-in — not a freeze, not a punishment, just a reminder that the door is always open if play has stopped being fun.

If you ever feel that your AgniClub activity is taking up more time, money or emotional bandwidth than you’re comfortable with, take a break. The platform will be here when you return. The deposit bonus, daily salary, referral super bonus and every free gift code will continue to be published exactly the same way, on exactly the same schedule, with exactly the same transparency.

How AgniClub protects your wallet

Every AgniClub login request is encrypted with TLS 1.3. Passwords are hashed using a modern, slow-by-design algorithm so that even in the unlikely event of a database leak, your password cannot be reversed. OTPs are delivered through reliable transactional SMS routes and expire in minutes, not hours. The in-app PIN is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers — losing your phone does not leak your PIN.

On the payments side, every deposit and every withdrawal is logged with a tamper-evident timestamp. The wallet history is yours to audit at any time and cannot be edited by support staff. If a credit ever looks wrong, you can produce the exact line item, the exact time, and the exact reference number when raising a ticket — which dramatically shortens resolution time.

Bank-binding is the single largest security upgrade most members make to their AgniClub account. Once the account is bound, withdrawals can only be sent to that bank. Even if an attacker somehow obtains your AgniClub login, they cannot redirect funds to a wallet they control. This is one of the reasons we make bank-binding mandatory for the deposit bonus and the referral super bonus.
