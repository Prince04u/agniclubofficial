How to reach AgniClub support

AgniClub support handles thousands of tickets every week. To get the fastest, most accurate answer, follow the simple structure below. Short, specific tickets are resolved in hours; long, multi-issue tickets take days because they have to be split across multiple specialist queues.

For most questions — login, agniclub register, agniclub giftcode redemption, missing deposit bonus, referral status, daily salary, withdrawals — email is the right channel. Our agents respond in chronological order and standard tickets receive a first reply within 12 hours, often much faster during business hours.

For verified agents, the dedicated agent line (agents@agniclub.com) takes priority and is monitored by senior staff. Use that line only for genuine agent-business questions — campaign reviews, ads-fee approvals, team-quality issues. Personal-account questions belong on the standard support address.

Support channels

Email — support@agniclub.com for general questions. Include your AgniClub user ID, the issue in a single sentence, and one screenshot if relevant.

Agent line — agents@agniclub.com for verified agents only. Include your agent ID, the campaign name (if applicable) and the exact reward tier you are referencing.

In-app support — Open the AgniClub application, tap Profile → Support, and start a chat. The in-app channel is best for time-sensitive issues like a stuck deposit or a failed OTP because it auto-attaches your account context.

Official broadcasts — for announcements, gift code drops and platform updates, follow the official AgniClub broadcast channels listed inside the app. Broadcasts are read-only and do not replace ticket-based support.

How to write a ticket that gets answered fast

Open with your AgniClub user ID. Don’t describe yourself by name — describe yourself by ID. The agent can pull your account in one click.

State the issue in a single sentence. Example: “Deposit bonus for ₹500 recharge on 14 June at 18:42 not credited.”

Attach exactly one screenshot. Multiple screenshots delay triage. One clear screenshot of the relevant screen is enough.

List the steps you already tried. Example: “Reinstalled app, re-logged in, refreshed wallet — bonus still missing.”

Avoid sending the same ticket twice. Duplicate tickets push your case to the back of the queue.

Response-time expectations

Standard tickets — first response within 12 hours, typical resolution within 24–48 hours.

Agent line — first response within 1 hour during business hours, typical resolution within 24 hours.

Withdrawal escalations — verified withdrawals are processed within one working day; escalations on stuck withdrawals receive priority routing.

Security incidents — suspected account takeovers, lost mobile-number access and similar urgent issues are handled by a dedicated security desk and typically resolved within 24–48 hours after identity verification.

What support will never ask for

Your password. Real AgniClub staff will never ask for your password under any circumstance.

Your OTP. The one-time password is yours alone. Anyone asking for it is impersonating support.

Your full bank card number or CVV. The platform never collects card details; deposits run through UPI/IMPS.

An advance fee to release a bonus or withdrawal. Every legitimate AgniClub reward is free to claim and free to withdraw.

How the AgniClub support queue actually works

Every ticket lands in a shared inbox that is triaged in chronological order during business hours and overnight by a smaller on-call team. The triage agent reads the subject line, the AgniClub user ID and the single screenshot, then routes the ticket to one of four queues: account, payments, agent, security. Each queue is staffed by specialists, and each queue has its own response-time targets. Most tickets receive their first reply within 12 hours; payments tickets often resolve faster because the wallet history makes the issue self-evident.

If your ticket touches multiple queues — say, a login problem and a missing deposit bonus — the triage agent splits it. You’ll receive two thread updates, one from each specialist. This is faster than forcing a single agent to learn both contexts, even though it can feel slower because you receive two replies instead of one.

The single biggest accelerator of resolution time is a precise screenshot. A blurry, cropped or oversized screenshot adds at least a back-and-forth round trip. A clear, full-screen screenshot with the relevant section in view often lets the specialist resolve the ticket on the first reply.

How to recognise an impersonator pretending to be AgniClub

Impersonators are unfortunately common in this category. They typically reach out via WhatsApp or random Telegram DMs, claim to be ‘AgniClub support’, and ask for your OTP, password, or a small ‘unlock fee’ to release a bonus. None of these are real. Real AgniClub support never initiates contact via WhatsApp, never asks for your OTP, never asks for your password, and never charges any fee to release any reward.

If you are unsure whether a message is genuine, do not reply. Instead, open the AgniClub application, tap Profile → Support, and ask the in-app chat directly: ‘Did your team just message me at [time]?’ The in-app channel is the authoritative source. If the answer is no, report the impersonator to support@agniclub.com with the subject line ‘Phishing report’ and a screenshot of the message. Our security desk blocks the source quickly and the report helps us track which messaging platforms attackers are using this month.

The cost of one minute of caution is essentially zero. The cost of giving an attacker your OTP can be your entire wallet. Always verify, never share.

More about how AgniClub treats members

Support is only one expression of how AgniClub treats its members. The sections below describe the wider culture — security, responsible play, transparent reward math and long-term focus — that the support team operates inside.

AgniClub responsible play commitments

Color prediction is meant to be entertainment, not income replacement. AgniClub publishes its responsible-play tooling directly inside the app and inside every reward page on this site so members are never more than a tap away from setting a healthy boundary. Personal deposit limits, session-length caps, daily cool-down windows and self-exclusion periods are all configurable from Profile → Responsible Play. None of these tools cost anything; none of them affect bonus eligibility; all of them can be tightened at any time without staff approval.

Our internal anti-fraud and welfare systems also work in the background. The platform flags abnormal betting curves, repeated late-night marathon sessions and sharp swings in deposit behaviour. When a flag triggers, our welfare desk reaches out privately with a soft check-in — not a freeze, not a punishment, just a reminder that the door is always open if play has stopped being fun.

If you ever feel that your AgniClub activity is taking up more time, money or emotional bandwidth than you’re comfortable with, take a break. The platform will be here when you return. The deposit bonus, daily salary, referral super bonus and every free gift code will continue to be published exactly the same way, on exactly the same schedule, with exactly the same transparency.

How AgniClub protects your wallet

Every AgniClub login request is encrypted with TLS 1.3. Passwords are hashed using a modern, slow-by-design algorithm so that even in the unlikely event of a database leak, your password cannot be reversed. OTPs are delivered through reliable transactional SMS routes and expire in minutes, not hours. The in-app PIN is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers — losing your phone does not leak your PIN.

On the payments side, every deposit and every withdrawal is logged with a tamper-evident timestamp. The wallet history is yours to audit at any time and cannot be edited by support staff. If a credit ever looks wrong, you can produce the exact line item, the exact time, and the exact reference number when raising a ticket — which dramatically shortens resolution time.

Bank-binding is the single largest security upgrade most members make to their AgniClub account. Once the account is bound, withdrawals can only be sent to that bank. Even if an attacker somehow obtains your AgniClub login, they cannot redirect funds to a wallet they control. This is one of the reasons we make bank-binding mandatory for the deposit bonus and the referral super bonus.

Trust signals and platform credibility

AgniClub publishes every reward number on this website — not in a hidden PDF, not in a Telegram message, not in a screenshot. The deposit bonus tiers, the new-player ladder, the agent referral bonus, the referral super bonus ladder all the way to ₹350,000, the daily salary thresholds and the ads-fee daily support amounts are all visible on the homepage. If a number isn’t printed here, it doesn’t exist on the platform.

We measure platform health publicly through three numbers shared with senior agents every month: round latency (target under 600 ms), withdrawal turnaround (target under one working day for verified accounts) and bonus-credit time (instant for deposit, end-of-day for daily salary). Those numbers are the contract we sign with every member who completes the agniclub register flow.

Independent of our internal metrics, agents themselves are an honest signal of platform quality. The AgniClub agent economy is intentionally generous because we believe a healthy agent layer is the only sustainable way to grow a color prediction platform. Strong agents stay only on platforms that pay reliably; the longer an agent has been active on AgniClub, the more confidence you can take in the platform.
