What is an AgniClub gift code?

An AgniClub gift code is a short alphanumeric string that credits bonus balance directly to your AgniClub wallet the moment you redeem it inside the application. Each code is single-use per account, time-limited, and capped at a specific number of total redemptions. When the cap is hit or the timer runs out, the code expires automatically — which is why we recommend bookmarking the Gift Code page and checking it daily.

Gift codes are part of the AgniClub reward ecosystem alongside the deposit bonus, the daily salary system and the referral super bonus. Unlike those programmes — which trigger automatically based on your activity — gift codes require you to take a tiny manual action: copy the code, open the in-app redemption box, paste, and tap Redeem. The credit lands in your wallet within a second.

We deliberately keep gift code amounts varied and irregular. Some codes credit a flat ₹10 to ₹50 to welcome new members. Others credit ₹100, ₹500 or more during festival drops, milestone celebrations or weekend events. Treat every published code as an opportunity — small amounts compound quickly when combined with the deposit bonus and the daily salary.

How to redeem an AgniClub gift code

Step 1 — Complete your AgniClub login. Gift codes can only be redeemed on a registered, signed-in account.

Step 2 — Open Wallet → Gift Code (or the “Redeem Code” shortcut on the home screen).

Step 3 — Paste the code exactly as published. Codes are case-sensitive and must not contain leading or trailing spaces.

Step 4 — Tap Redeem. A success popup confirms the credited amount and the new wallet balance.

Step 5 — Verify the credit in your wallet history. Every gift code redemption appears as a separate line item with the timestamp, the code used and the exact amount.

Rules and limits for AgniClub gift codes

One redemption per account per code. The same code cannot be used twice from the same AgniClub login.

One account per person. Attempting to redeem the same code from multiple accounts on the same device, IP, bank or mobile number disqualifies every account involved and forfeits any pending bonus.

Codes are case-sensitive. AGNI100 and agni100 are not the same code. Always copy-paste rather than retyping.

Codes can expire by time or by cap. A code may say “valid for 24 hours” but still expire earlier if the total redemption cap is reached. Redeeming quickly is the right strategy.

Gift code balance follows the standard wallet rules. It can be used to place predictions immediately and counts towards betting volume for the referral super bonus and daily salary thresholds.

Never buy gift codes from third parties. Any code sold outside the official AgniClub channels is either fake or already redeemed. We will not refund balance lost to third-party scams.

Tips for getting the most out of AgniClub gift codes

How AgniClub protects your wallet

Every AgniClub login request is encrypted with TLS 1.3. Passwords are hashed using a modern, slow-by-design algorithm so that even in the unlikely event of a database leak, your password cannot be reversed. OTPs are delivered through reliable transactional SMS routes and expire in minutes, not hours. The in-app PIN is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers — losing your phone does not leak your PIN.

On the payments side, every deposit and every withdrawal is logged with a tamper-evident timestamp. The wallet history is yours to audit at any time and cannot be edited by support staff. If a credit ever looks wrong, you can produce the exact line item, the exact time, and the exact reference number when raising a ticket — which dramatically shortens resolution time.

Bank-binding is the single largest security upgrade most members make to their AgniClub account. Once the account is bound, withdrawals can only be sent to that bank. Even if an attacker somehow obtains your AgniClub login, they cannot redirect funds to a wallet they control. This is one of the reasons we make bank-binding mandatory for the deposit bonus and the referral super bonus.
